I'm glad to see that [MacVim 183](https://github.com/macvim-dev/macvim/releases/tag/release-183), released on April 8th, contains the [fix for the issue](https://github.com/vim/vim/issues/18829) that I reported and that got quickly fixed in regular Vim's repo in November.  Netrw was [throwing an annoying error](/content/blog/2025/12/09/vim-netrw-sftp-error.md) every time the directory was changed over SFTP, slowing things down a lot.  Now I can SFTP from MacVim again much more smoothly.
