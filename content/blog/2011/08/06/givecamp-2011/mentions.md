Several days later, Mark W. Schumann, who I believe is the master of ceremonies, for lack of a better term, for GiveCamp, commented:

> I like it that we had such a mix of arts, service, cultural, advocacy, and health organizations. You'll see more GiveCamp successes all over the Cleveland area in coming years.
